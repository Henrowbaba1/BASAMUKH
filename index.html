Cultivating Excellence

With over 15 years of industry experience, Basamukh Seed Integrated Services is a premier agricultural enterprise based in Samaru Zaria, Kaduna.

We specialize in the production, processing, and distribution of high-quality seeds that drive agricultural productivity across Africa. Our commitment lies in bridging the gap between potential and performance, ensuring every farmer has access to seeds that resist disease, adapt to local conditions, and deliver maximum yield.

Our Capacity

  • Deliver large volumes of certified seeds within agreed timelines.
  • Maintain strict quality control and compliance standards.
  • Manage end-to-end seed production, processing, and distribution.
