Cultivating Excellence

With over 15 years of industry experience, Basamukh Seed Integrated Services is a premier agricultural enterprise based in Samaru Zaria, Kaduna.

We specialize in the production, processing, and distribution of high-quality seeds that drive agricultural productivity across Africa. Our commitment lies in bridging the gap between potential and performance, ensuring every farmer has access to seeds that resist disease, adapt to local conditions, and deliver maximum yield.

Past Projects & Contract Execution

A proven track record of delivering high-quality seeds to government programs and international organizations. All engagements were executed successfully with full delivery and payment completion.

Kaduna State Agricultural Development Programme (KADP)

The Company was engaged as a contracted seed supplier under the Kaduna State Agricultural Development Programme, where we supplied certified, high-quality seeds in large quantities to support cluster-based farming initiatives in the Ruwan Sanyi and Ludduga clusters. Our delivery contributed to improved crop productivity and the contract was fulfilled with successful delivery and full payment.

Kebbi State Agricultural Development Programme

Under a formal supply arrangement, we provided bulk quantities of improved seeds to support agricultural production programs in Kebbi State. Our seeds met required quality and certification standards, ensuring enhanced crop performance for beneficiary farmers. The contract was delivered efficiently, and all financial obligations were honoured upon completion.

Pyxera Global – Rayuwa Project, Katsina State

The Company served as a contracted supplier for the Rayuwa Project implemented by Pyxera Global, delivering large volumes of certified seeds to project beneficiaries across Katsina State. Our role focused on ensuring timely availability of quality inputs to farmers participating in the program, executed to full specification.

Nuru International – Adamawa State

In partnership with Nuru International, our company supplied high-quality agricultural seeds at large scale to support community-based agricultural interventions. Our seeds contributed to improved farm yields and livelihoods within target communities, with all deliverables met and payments received as agreed.

Gombe State Government – Agricultural Input Supply Programme

The company successfully executed a contract for the supply of bulk quantities of certified, high-quality seeds to the Gombe State Government in support of its agricultural development and food security initiatives. This included Wet Season distribution and support for the Rural Access and Agricultural Marketing Project (RAAMP).

Strategic Partnerships

Collaborating with leading research and regulatory institutions to ensure genetic purity and excellence.

National Agricultural Seed Council (NASC)

We operate under the regulatory oversight of NASC to ensure compliance with seed certification standards, quality control, and genetic purity throughout our production processes.

Institute for Agricultural Research (IAR)

Our collaboration with IAR provides access to breeder and foundation seeds, as well as technical expertise in seed cleaning, processing, and quality assurance, forming the backbone of our multiplication system.

National Cereals Research Institute (NCRI), Badeggi

We source high-quality foundation seeds for key crops including rice and soybean from NCRI. This ensures access to scientifically developed, high-yielding, and adaptable seed varieties, guaranteeing consistency in performance.

Eco-Basic Seed Company

We partner with Eco-Basic Seeds for improved varieties including PBR Cowpea and hybrid maize, enabling us to supply climate-resilient and high-yielding seed options to the Nigerian market.

Value Seeds Limited Company

Value Seeds Limited supports our operations through the provision of foundation seeds across multiple crop categories, ensuring continuity in our production pipeline and product availability.

African Agricultural Technology Foundation (AATF)

Through AATF, we participate in the production of certified PBR seeds, including advanced and biotech varieties, strengthening our contribution to agricultural innovation and food security.
